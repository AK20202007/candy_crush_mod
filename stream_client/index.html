Stream camera → laptop (thin client)

If the page spins forever, open http://…/ping on the phone first (same host/port as this app). You should see {"status":"ok"} in under a second. If not, the phone cannot reach your laptop (wrong IP, different Wi‑Fi, or macOS Firewall blocking Python).

Mobile browsers require a secure context for camera (HTTPS or localhost). For camera access over Wi‑Fi, use TLS (e.g. ngrok http 8765) and open the https URL on the phone.
